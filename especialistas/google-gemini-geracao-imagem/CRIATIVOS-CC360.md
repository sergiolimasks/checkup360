---

## Instrucoes Gerais

1. **Idioma do prompt**: Ingles (resultados mais precisos)
2. **Preset**: "Design" no ImageFX, ou Gemini Pro 3.1 no chat
3. **Magic Prompt**: Desligado (controle literal)
4. **Texto na imagem**: NAO pedir — adicionar depois no Canva
5. **Gerar 4-6 variacoes** de cada prompt e selecionar a melhor
6. **Iteracao**: Se 80% bom, use o chat pra ajustar ("make the lighting darker", "zoom in closer")

## DICAS DE ITERACAO NO GEMINI

Se o resultado ficou 80% bom, NAO recomece do zero. Use o chat:

- Camera longe demais: `"Zoom in much closer, I want to see only from chest up, face should fill 70% of the frame"`
- Fundo estranho: `"Keep the person exactly the same, but replace the background with a plain solid dark gray wall, nothing else visible"`
- Iluminacao flat: `"Make the lighting much more dramatic, strong side light from the left only, deep shadows on the right"`
- Muito saturado: `"Desaturate the colors significantly, I want muted film-like tones similar to Kodak Portra 400"`
- Expressao errada: `"Keep everything the same but change the expression to more worried and frustrated, furrowed brow"`
